Screens OS

Operating system focused on security, modernity and newbie friendly. Based on Linux and ZeroNet, with HTML5 interface/apps

Simple and easy to use, but advanced

Screens combines both worlds: newbie-friendly, and advanced features.

It haves user levels (newbie, beginer, medium, advanced) and built-in computing courses, offering Open Badges as learning incentive.

Based on Linux

The same Linux/Debian apps you already use, can be imported and used on Screens. The security of Linux you know, but improved.

HTML5 UI and apps

Simple HTML5 UI, that is automatically updated using ZeroNet. Also, alongside Linux CLI programs and Debian apps/snaps, modern & lightweight HTML5 apps with auto-updates.

Chrome extensions/apps and Ethereum dApps can also be supported.

ZeroNet

The gentle version of deep web, with P2P distributed sites that supports user login without PHP.

Open zites on browser using zero:// protocol or use ZeroNet apps. You can also use built-in Namecoin to register .bit domains.

Easily create your first site or app

With Eventlr and FrameworkJS, you can easily create sites and apps with visual event sheets for programming without text-languages knowledge.

Type faster

Screens haves support and tutorials for Colemak keyboard layout.

Run Android and Linux apps

With Anbox and Wine already set-up, you can run the apps from other OS you already use.

MacOS/iOS apps could also be supported.

Best setup for developers

Pre-installed Git, Git LFS, NodeJS, Electron, Cordova, Eventlr, FrameworkJS (with VueJS) and other dev tools.

It also haves a easy-to-use game engine that even a kid can use, that is like Construct 2/Eventlr: using visual event sheets.

Care about your identy

Create your profile, and verify all your works with Keybase/OpenPGP/Bitcoin signatures.

Care about your works

Use Namecoin to register "copyrights".

Chat with privacy

With Riot.im, a Discord alternative

Quantum-resistant money

With Quantum Resistant Ledger, a Bitcoin alternative that is designed to be even more resistant to quantum computing

Connect to your friends and neighbors

With CJDNS/YouNet/ZeroNet, you can access offline sites and send offline messages.

Open Badges

Show to other users on your profile, what are your efforts and achievements when using Screens and its apps.

Best OS for videos

With Kdenlive editor, OBS Studio (for screenrecording and livestreamming) and Video Script Maker, give to your video production a next level.
